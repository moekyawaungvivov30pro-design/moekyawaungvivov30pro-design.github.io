About Me

Designing digital experiences that matter

Hi, I'm Moe Kyaw Aung — a multi-disciplinary designer and developer based in Myanmar. I have a deep passion for creating digital products that are both visually stunning and incredibly functional.

I believe great design is where strategy meets artistry. My workflow combines rigorous user research with expressive visual thinking — the result is work that resonates with real people and achieves real goals.

Whether you need a brand built from scratch, a web app engineered with care, or a design system that scales — I'm your person.
